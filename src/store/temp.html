Full Job Description:

LabQ Diagnostics is a full-service, independent clinical laboratory servicing New York, New Jersey, and Pennsylvania that provides exceptional quality service with a quick turnaround time. As we continue to grow our company, we are searching to build a strong and motivated team, with members who desire to be a part of constructing an exceptional facility.

Education and Qualifications:

  • Associate’s or Bachelor's degree in science preferred
  • Organized, attention to detail
  • Previous experience in clinical laboratory.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Assists in the laboratory by cleaning, washing, and disinfecting laboratory equipment and workbenches.
  • Plays supportive roles to technologists and technicians.
  • Helps with transferring specimens from a primary tube into a secondary tube and/or sample aliquoting.
  • Works directly under the lab supervisors.
  • Works with administrative and clerical work associates in the lab ordering supplies and filing records.
  • Gathers and transports specimens from the accessioning area to the appropriate technical department.
  • Files instrument printouts after the tests have been performed.
  • Files patient results print outs and calibration, QC documentation.
  • Scans completed samples into the scanning system
  • Helps when supplies arrive and have to be organized, labeled and stored.
  • Works in an organized manner in accordance with established laboratory policies and procedures.

Job Types: Full-time

Salary: $18.00 – $23.00 per hour

Work Remotely: No

Work Location: One location
